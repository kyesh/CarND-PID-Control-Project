The P component was used to turn the vehicle in the direction it needed to go to return to the center of the road.

The D component was used to limit how quickly the vehile approched the center of the road so it would not oscillate or overshoot.

The I component was used to help give an extra turning boost while in turns which created prologend periods of being outside of the center of the lane.
